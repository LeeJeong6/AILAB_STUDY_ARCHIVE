Paper Seminar

GroupViT: Semantic Segmentation Emerges from Text Supervision

Date
March 20, 2026
Presenter
Jeongik Lee
Venue
CVPR 2022
Authors
Xu et al.
Keywords
zero-shot segmentation vision-language grouping tokens CLIP ViT
Abstract

GroupViT is a hierarchical grouping ViT model that learns pixel-level semantic segmentation with only text supervision. It introduces grouping blocks that progressively merge images into meaningful groups, achieving zero-shot segmentation without any dense annotations.

Key Points
  • Learn visual segments bottom-up through grouping tokens
  • Trained with CLIP-like image-text contrastive loss
  • Achieves 52.3% mIoU on PASCAL VOC (zero-shot)
  • No dense annotations required whatsoever
